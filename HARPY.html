HARPY (THE MIME):

This competition features talented performers who bring emotions and narratives to life through expressive body language, gestures, and facial expressions without a single word. Each act delivers a unique and captivating take on life's moments, from the humorous to the deeply moving. The audience will be swept into a world where silence speaks volumes, ensuring an unforgettable and exhilarating experience.

RULES:
1.Topic: Sustainable Development Goals. 
2.Time Limit: 5+1 Minutes
3. No spoken language allowed.
4. Facial and Physical expressions only.
5. No physical contact with audience.
6. Appropriate Usage of props.
7. Any kind of vulgarity will lead to disqualification.
